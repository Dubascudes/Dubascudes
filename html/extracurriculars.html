Summer, 2018:

While in Quetico Provincial Park, Ontario, I lead a crew on an 9 day, 75 mile trek. During this trek, we paddled in canoe the majority of the way, and carried 100% of our food and gear into the park. No trace of our presence was left.


Summer, 2018:

I completed my Eagle project in May of 2018, and I was awarded the rank of Eagle scout that summer. For my project, I designed, raised funds for, and lead the construction of two agility courses for the St. Johns Country Pet center. This county program is a no-kill shelter, and is responsible for the care of many dogs. The purpose of this project was to give the dogs activities and challenges as an outlet for their energy, built up from being in a cage all day. This energy often times deters potential rescuers.
